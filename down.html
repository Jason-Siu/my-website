Download and try to beat my TICTACTOE AI
  • I made this during the summer after my second year in computer science when I wanted to learn more about AI with games. I decided to choose tictactoe because it is a very simple game. It is unbeatable, and I have had other people try to beat it, but they ended up tying, or sometimes even losing! When you open the file, there should be weirdly named folder (it only helps me make it stand out even more), and in that folder there will be the .html, .css, and .js files. Open the html file on your main browser, and you should be able to play against the AI.
Download and try to beat my CHESS AI
  • This took a very long time. It took time to get used to the chess.js library. However, eventually I got the hang of it. I am not that good at chess, so this AI can beat me easily. I would say that if you are a decent chess player, that you can possible beat this. However, for the beginner like me, it is unlikely. There is also an option to make the AI versus itself. If you are able to change script.js file, there is at the last line where if you get rid of the comment to that function call, you are able to make the AI play against itself. Now, I am not sure if this is for any reason, but whenever the AI plays against itself, it runs faster than when a human plays against it. That is weird. Again, just test it by opening the .html file onto your browser. When you play against it, depending on your computer speed, be patient. It it looks like it froze, keep waiting, its just doing more calculations.
